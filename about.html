About Us

Welcome to our shop! My name is Christian Honrejas, and I'm pround to be the owner of this space where passion for coffee and community come together.

This shop began with a simple idea: to create a place where people can slow down, enjoy great coffee, and feel a genuine sense of connection. coffee, for me, has always been more than just a drink it's a daily ritual that brings comfort and brings people closer. that's the heart behind everything we do here.
